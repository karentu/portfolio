About

Have you ever needed to find a study buddy but couldn't? Or found yourself alone at 4am in the library? Here's a solution to those lonely nights! Gaggle could be described as "Tinder for study buddies" and will help you find other people who are working on similar subjects or near you.

Users enter their name, course CRN, and contact information. The program automatically pinpoints users' locations and offers them a display of nearby users studying for the same class. Users can then choose to connect with users they see. If the other user accepts, their contact information would be sent to each other.

Design Principles

We wanted to evoke fun and light-hearted emotions when people used this site. After all, we imagine that the times that users will turn to our app may be dark indeed. In our design, we intentionally chose bright primary and secondary colors in order to

Despite the jovial tone of the website design, we wanted to keep the user interface relatively distraction free and simple. Especially since users are inputting some personal information into the site, we felt that this design choice was necessary in order to ensure that our users felt secure in telling the website what their phone number and courses were.

In terms of user optimization, we optimized the website for speedy onboarding and easy navigation. Especially when one's stressed, having a website that requires dozens of steps of onboarding is the worst. Eventually, we intend to create a authentication/account system so users don't need to repeatedly input the same information.
